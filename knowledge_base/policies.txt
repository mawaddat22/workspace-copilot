=== WORKSPACE POLICIES ===

OFFICE HOURS:
The workspace is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
On weekends, the space is open from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

CANCELLATION POLICY:
Bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund.
Cancellations made within 24 hours are non-refundable.

MEETING ROOMS:
Meeting rooms can be booked for a minimum of 1 hour and a maximum of 8 hours per day.
Rooms must be cleaned and vacated 10 minutes before the next booking.

DESK BOOKING:
Hot desks are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
Dedicated desks can be reserved monthly.

AMENITIES:
All memberships include high-speed WiFi, printing (up to 50 pages/month),
coffee and tea, and access to common areas.
Premium memberships also include access to private phone booths and lockers.

MEMBERSHIP PLANS:
- Basic: Hot desk access, weekdays only — $99/month
- Standard: Hot desk access, all days — $149/month
- Premium: Dedicated desk + all amenities — $299/month

MAINTENANCE:
Report any maintenance issues to the front desk or via the AI copilot.
Issues are typically resolved within 24-48 hours.

GUEST POLICY:
Members may bring up to 2 guests per visit. Guests must sign in at reception.
Guest day passes are available for $15/day.

PAYMENT:
Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month.
Payment is due within 7 days of invoice generation.
Accepted payment methods: credit card, bank transfer.
